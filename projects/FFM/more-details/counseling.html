Exchanged Life Counseling

Exchanged Life Counseling is offered through Faith Fellowship Ministries. Our Pastor, Gordon "Wes" Young, earned his master's degree in counseling from Luther Rice University. He leads our team in assisting our community in achieving an "exchanged life" transitioning from worldly tension to a stress-free, glorious life, equipped to handle life's challenges.


Our success rate stands at 95% for those who are committed to achieving victory in their lives and in their families. Whether you're dealing with substance addiction, family stress, financial challenges, or other issues, we are here to help. This ministry is rooted in and founded on the Word of God.


If you are ready to take your life seriously and pursue a path of victory, please allow us to assist you.
