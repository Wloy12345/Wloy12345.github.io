Our Services

  • Traditional Worship

    Join us for our Traditional Worship service every Sunday at 10:45 AM. Experience the timeless beauty of traditional hymns, liturgy, and religious traditions. Our Traditional Worship service is a celebration of faith, steeped in the rich history of our church. It offers a peaceful and reverent atmosphere for reflection and devotion.

  • In-Depth Bible Study

    Join us for our In-Depth Bible Study every Wednesday at 6:30 PM. Dive deep into the teachings of the Bible with our comprehensive Bible study sessions. Explore the scriptures, ask questions, and deepen your faith. This is a great opportunity to engage in meaningful discussions, gain spiritual insights, and connect with others who share your passion for the Word of God.

  • Contemporary Services

    Join us for our Contemporary Services held every Sunday at 10:45 AM. Experience the vibrancy of contemporary worship with a modern twist. Our services feature uplifting music, engaging messages, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're new to faith or looking for a fresh perspective, our Contemporary Services provide a dynamic and inclusive community where you can explore and celebrate your spirituality.

  • Counseling Services

    Our dedicated counciling is here to provide support and guidance to individuals and families facing life's most difficult challenges.

Mission and vision

Faith Fellowship Ministries is a Community Church that was founded on being a New Testament based Church. Our mission is to grow in God through His word in order to share His love with the community. We pray that we can make a difference in the lives of individuals and families with whom we have contact. We do this through our fellowship on Sundays, our Christian school, and “Exchanged Life Counseling”. Our vision is to be humble, and grow together as we follow Jesus’ example sharing His love, one for another.
