About Us

Who We Are

At Pumpkin Patch, we are a leading provider of crop management solutions. With years of experience in the industry, we strive to make crop management easier and more efficient for farmers and growers. Our innovative application offers comprehensive tools and features to assist you in every aspect of crop growth. From monitoring, analysis, planning and optimization. We are dedicated to helping you achieve optimal results and maximize your harvest. Trust Pumpkin Patch for all your crop management needs.

Our Team

We have a dedicated team of professionals who are passionate about crop management and helping farmers and growers succeed. With expertise in various fields, our team works together to provide innovative solutions and support to our customers. From crop specialists to software developers, we are committed to delivering the best crop management experience. Meet our team and discover the expertise and dedication behind PK Patch.
